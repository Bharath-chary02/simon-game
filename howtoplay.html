How to Play

Objective:

Repeat the color sequence shown by the game. Each level, the sequence gets longer. Try to go as far as you can!

How to Start:

  • Click the "Start" button (or press any key).
  • The game will flash a random colored box.

How to Play:

  1. Watch carefully: A box will flash. Remember the color.
  2. Click the same box to repeat the color.
  3. After each level, one more color will be added to the sequence.
  4. You must click the colors in the exact order as the game shows.

If You Make a Mistake:

  • The screen will flash red.
  • The game ends immediately.
  • Your final score is shown on the screen.

Restart:

Click the "Restart" button to start again from Level 1.

Replay:

You can also press any key or click the "Replay" button again to play a fresh game.
